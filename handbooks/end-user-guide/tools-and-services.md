# Tools and Services

A tour of the member services and personal tools available in [FORCA] Command Grid. Some
of these are audience-controlled by leadership (they may be open to allies or the public
too); all are available to members.

## Freight

`/freight/` — Price a haul on the rate calculator, then post it as an outstanding contract.
Any corp hauler can claim it, fly it, and mark it delivered for ISK. If you're the one
flying, claim a posted job, complete the in-game courier contract, and confirm delivery in
the app — your delivery is cross-checked against the real contract before you get full
credit.

## Buyback

`/buyback/` — Paste a list of items to get an instant appraisal, priced off Jita sell with
a location haircut for highsec/lowsec/nullsec risk. Happy with the number? Post it to the
**offer board** and a corpmate buys the lot and settles with you in-game. Some corps also
run a corp-funded **guaranteed** buyback queue for a faster, no-negotiation sale — look for
that option on the buyback page if your corp has it enabled.

## Corp Store

`/store/` — Order a ready-to-fly doctrine fit or a made-to-order hull up through capitals,
secured with an upfront deposit. Doctrine fits and sub-capital hulls are priced off live
Jita sell plus a modest markup; capital and supercapital hulls are priced off their
estimated build cost plus a per-class profit margin leaders set in the store settings. Your
order lands on the corp's fulfilment board, where a member (not you) claims it, builds or
buys it, and delivers it to you by in-game contract. Track your own orders from
`/store/orders/mine/`.

## Industry Center

`/industry/` — The unified home for production planning: a manufacturing calculator, a T2
invention planner, a production-chain explorer, a blueprint browser, and a job tracker, all
driven by live market prices. Save a multi-item project, let it recompute the full bill of
materials, generate a shopping list, reserve corp stock against it, and push it into
trackable build jobs. Every estimate is explicit about the assumptions behind it (market
source, tax, facility).

## Mining ("My mining")

`/mining/` — Your personal view into the corp's mining ledger, pulled from ESI refinery
observer records: what you mined, when, and its Jita value. Corp-wide ledger management,
tax rates, and payout splits are handled by leadership; this is your own self-scoped slice
of it.

## Planetary Industry

`/industry/pi/` — A guided Planetary Industry planner: it teaches you PI, walks you
through a plan wizard across your planets, and produces a transparent, price-driven profit
estimate (chains, taxes, hauling). It's advisory only — it never touches your colonies in
the game — though you can optionally import your live colonies so the app can
health-monitor your plan against them.

## Navigation and jump tools

`/tools/` — A self-hosted route planner (gate routes), a hull-aware cyno jump planner
(including safe high-sec-exit handling and fuel math), a jump-range reach finder, region
and universe maps, per-system dossiers, and PvP intel. If your corp maintains a network of
Ansiblex jump bridges and cyno beacons, the planners route through it automatically.

## Stockpile and assets

`/stockpile/` — See corp target stockpiles at key locations and what's short, and (if
you've opted in) your own personal ESI-synced assets grouped by location and value. You can
also post and claim member-run hauling jobs to move stock where it's needed.

## Tasks

`/tasks/` — The corp's shared execution board. Every gap leadership identifies — a supply
shortfall, an industry job, a doctrine rollout step — becomes a task, either assigned to
someone or open for anyone to claim. Completing one credits you in your
[contribution ledger](#contribution-ledger).

## Contribution ledger

`/pilots/contributions/` — Your personal "what I did for the corp" record, in native units
(kills, hauls delivered, jobs built, and so on) rather than a single combined score. It
feeds the [Hall of Fame](./combat-and-progression.md#hall-of-fame) and a recognition feed;
you can opt in or out of appearing in corp-wide recognition from here.

## Knowledge base

`/kb/` — Versioned corp documentation. Some pages are public (useful for recruiting),
some are member-only, and some are officer-only; the page itself tells you which. A few
pages embed live snippets of your own readiness or SRP status so the guide never goes
stale on you.

## Raffles

`/raffle/` — Engagement raffles where you earn tickets from real activity — PvP, mining,
fleet attendance, or a manual award from leadership — and winners are drawn with a public,
verifiable **commit-reveal** process so nobody (including leadership) can rig the draw.
Check your own ticket count and history from a raffle's own page, and opt out of raffle
nudges if you'd rather not be reminded.

## Mentorship (as a cadet)

`/mentorship/` — If you're new, register as a mentee and get paired with an experienced
corpmate (leadership may pair you manually, or the system can suggest a match). Together
you work through structured learning tracks and field exercises; your progress is
validated honestly against your own already-synced data, and completing exercises earns
cosmetic badges plus a chance at an officer-approved reward. See your own progress and
pairing at `/mentorship/me/`.
