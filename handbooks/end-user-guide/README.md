# End-User Guide

Welcome to **[FORCA] Command Grid** — your corporation's operations hub. This guide is
written for line pilots, newbros, corp members, and anyone considering joining the
corporation. If you fly with us (or are thinking about it), this is your handbook.

## Who this guide is for

- **Corp members** — pilots whose character is in the home corporation. Most of the app
  is built for you: readiness, fleets, industry, SRP, and more.
- **Newbros** — brand-new pilots who just joined. Start with
  [Getting started](./getting-started.md); the app is designed to make your first weeks
  easy.
- **Prospective recruits and visitors** — you can browse the public killboard and public
  knowledge base, and see the onboarding surface, before you ever join.

If you are one of the corp's leaders (director, officer, FC, recruiter, or mentor) running
the app rather than just using it, see the
[Administrator Handbook](../administrator-handbook/README.md) instead.

## What [FORCA] Command Grid is

Think of it as your corp's mission control: a single website that ties together your
combat record, your ship readiness, fleet sign-ups, industry planning, member services
(freight, buyback, the corp store), and all the small tools that make flying with the
corporation easier — all built on your own EVE Online character data, shared only with
your consent.

## What a member can do

Once you have signed in and your character is confirmed as a corp member, you get:

- A personal **Command Center dashboard** that pulls together everything relevant to you
  today.
- Your place on the **killboard**, your **combat rank**, and your progress toward the next
  one.
- A **readiness** view showing what you can fly right now, and what to train or buy next.
- The ability to **sign up for fleets**, claim a ship slot, and get credit for showing up.
- Access to member services: **freight**, **buyback**, the **corp store**, the
  **Industry Center**, **mining**, **Planetary Industry**, and **navigation tools**.
- A place to file an **SRP claim** if you lose a ship in an approved op.
- A **contribution ledger** recording what you've done for the corp, feeding the monthly
  **Hall of Fame**.
- Control over your own **ESI data-sharing scopes**, your **notification channels**, and
  your **privacy/data rights**.
- The ability to use the app in **your own language**, where leadership has enabled it:
  English plus up to eight others.
