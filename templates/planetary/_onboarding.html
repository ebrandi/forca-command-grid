What is Planetary Industry?

Passive ISK from the ground up

You deploy colonies on planets. Extractor planets pull raw resources and refine them; factory planets combine materials into higher-value goods. Reset the extractors on your own schedule, haul the output to market, and it earns while you're doing other things. Taxes, hauling and market prices decide whether it's worth it — this planner does that maths for you.
