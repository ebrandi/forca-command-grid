Emergency grant override

Keep this OFF. When enabled, it lets a Director grant tickets to a pilot who is not enrolled in FORCA or has no valid ESI token — bypassing the core rule that only enrolled, ESI-connected pilots earn tickets and can win.

Every such grant is audited and still needs the Director role plus a per-grant confirmation. Turn it on only for a genuine, one-off emergency, then turn it straight back off.
