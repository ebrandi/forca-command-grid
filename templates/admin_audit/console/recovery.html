Account recovery

When a pilot sells or transfers a character (or their character predates owner-hash capture), login fails closed and tells them to contact an officer. Detaching a character clears its owner hash, unlinks it from its account and revokes its tokens, so the true owner can re-link it at their next EVE login. Every detach is audited.
