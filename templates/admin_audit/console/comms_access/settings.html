This subsystem drives external-platform roles from corp membership + RBAC. It ships inert and is configured entirely here — no server access needed. To go live: (1) enter the Discord credentials, (2) enable the feature, (3) arm the platform, and (4) take its role mappings out of dry-run. The sync only ever touches roles that appear on the Role mappings page.
